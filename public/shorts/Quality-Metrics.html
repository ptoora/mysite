Parsh's Testing Wiki - Quality Metrics

The purpose of quality metrics is to provide a trigger for revisiting the SDLC to explore potential weaknesses. Quality is about; defects, timeliness, and cost effectiveness

Headline Metrics

Metric Description Action
Count of bugs that reached Production Measure the quality of exploratory testing and coverage of regression tests May need to review the time alloted to exploraory testing, and review the techniques employed, and the test coverage
Count of bugs that reached exploratry testing Measure of the quality of the acceptance criteria and their coverage in the automated tests. May need to improve Story refinement and/or the test automation review process
Cycle Time - time from story pick-up to story ready-for-release Measure of the efficiency and timeliness of the process May need to improve processes and increase automation
Time To Recovery - time needed to fallback Measure of the ability to recover in a timely fashion May need to improve processes and increase automation

Engineering Metrics

Metric Description Action
Code coverage in Unit tests Confidence that that the app is robust May need to increase coverage and enforce in the build
Code coverage in Acceptance tests Confidence that we have sufficient Acceptance coverage May need to increase coverage and enforce in the build
Continuouse Integration build and test time Support fail fast and is the lifeblood of an Agile way of working May need to invest in speeding up the process through parallelisation, stubbing etc
