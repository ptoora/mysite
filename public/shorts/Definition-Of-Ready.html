Parsh's Testing Wiki - Definition Of ready

The 'Defintion Of Ready' describes the prerequisites for a Story being played. It is as important, if not more, than the Defintion Of Ready.

Before a story is picked-up for development, the following should be in place:

  1. a set of comprehesive Acceptance Criteria reviewed by; the QA, the Dev, and the Business
  2. a small sized story that is realistically deliverable into Prod by the end of the Sprint
  3. consideration given to; performance, security, seo, devops (logging), data analytics, accessibility, and testability
