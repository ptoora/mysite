Parsh's Testing Wiki - 'Going to the FLICS' - a simple approach to efficient testing

For every component (service or front-end) in your architecture, consider applying a FLICS testing approach:

F = functional testing with 3rd parties stubbed
L = local state testing, component chains in your domain, with 3rd parties stubbed
I = integration testing with real 3rd party clients (run a small subset of functional)
C = consumer testing initiated (run a small set of their functional)
S = system state testing, component chains across domains, with real third parties (smallest set)

P.S. Don't forget; Performance, Security, SEO and Accessibility
